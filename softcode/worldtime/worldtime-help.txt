%chSyntax%cn
%t+worldtime
%t+worldtime <ZONE or PLACE>

%chExamples%cn
%t+worldtime
%t+worldtime PDT
%t+worldtime Egypt
%t+worldtime +1300

%chDescription%cn

%tDisplays the current time in various timezones throughout the world. The server time is listed in red. Universal time is listed in cyan.  Other common timezones are listed in white.

Worldtimes idea courtesy Veit@HM. Code by Chime@HM. Zone data by Chime@HM from Wikipedia.
